This initiative was aimed at creating a website for online hotel booking. I was responsible for the database design and back-end development, ensuring that all features were integrated and functioned together seamlessly.

The website allows users to watch room, news, about us(ad web), and manage their accounts. The website also has an admin page for managing hotel, users, and other data.
